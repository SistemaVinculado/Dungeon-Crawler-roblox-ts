CHANGELOG

Note: This game is in Alpha. Features and balance are subject to change. Thank you for playing!

v2.0.0 Alpha - The Ascension Update

  • MASSIVE FEATURE - 9-Tier Rarity System: Loot has been completely overhauled! Items now drop in 9 tiers of rarity: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic, Artifact, Divine, and Exotic. Higher rarities are visually distinct and signify greater power.
  • UI Overhaul: The Inventory, Shop, and Forge have been updated to display rarity colors, making it easier to identify your best gear at a glance.
  • World Progression System: Unlock new worlds with unique themes and enemies by defeating the boss of the previous world. The adventure begins in the Dark Forest and continues into the Crystal Caves!
  • Character & Skill System: Choose between the Warrior and Mage classes, each with a unique skill tree. Level up to gain skill points and unlock powerful active abilities and passive bonuses.
  • Dynamic Dungeons: Explore procedurally generated dungeons shrouded in a Fog of War. Discover hidden traps, healing fountains, and treasure chests while hunting down enemies.
  • Tactical Combat: Engage in turn-based combat with a variety of monsters, each with unique behaviors. Manage your Health and Energy, and exploit enemy weaknesses.
  • Meta-Progression:
    • Forge: Use Arcane Dust to upgrade your equipment.
    • Shop: Buy and sell items, with a junk system and buyback feature for convenience.
    • Appearance: Customize your hero with cosmetic skins.
    • Codex, Leaderboard & Quests: Track your discoveries, compete for high scores, and see available quests (coming soon!).
  • Difficulty Tiers: Conquer dungeons on multiple difficulties, from Easy to the unlockable Dark+, for greater challenges and rewards.
